Tenants / Workspaces

Create Organizations and Businesses first, then tenant workspaces can be mapped to those records in the next step. Tenant-level behavior remains the operational workspace/location boundary.

Add organization and business

Use Organization for the umbrella account and Business for the operating brand. This does not change existing tenant/location behavior yet.

Add tenant workspace

The slug is used as the tenant workspace's unique short name. Leave it blank to generate one from the tenant name. Logos are uploaded to Cloudflare R2 under Tenant Logos/.
